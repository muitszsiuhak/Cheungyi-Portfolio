SCULPTURE / POSTHUMANISM

"Parrot-Lobster-Crab"

Instructor: David Ruy | Assistant Teacher: Esin Karaosman



The Nectomoebus is a hybrid parrot-amoeba that thrives by inhabiting scavenged marine corpses, repurposing the remains as both sustenance and shelter, are among this extraordinary catalog that straddles the boundary between the familiar and the uncanny.


Garden of Unfamiliar Surfaces

These artificially generated creatures are much like “synthetic taxidermies” - they are not much more than optical manipulation crafted through rasterized pixel arrangement of existing surfaces. In this “Garden of Surfaces”, the exterior once again takes agency, regardless of its form, origin, or meaning. They are vastly different, yet oddly similar... Unexpected possibilities are then being unveiled.


medium: polylactic acid/ silicone/ resin/ epoxy/ polyclay/ artificial feathers/ acrylic paint/ cotton/ wool


sketch: interactive motion graphics
