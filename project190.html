Wood of Splines

Instructor: Damjan Jovanovic | Teammate: Courtney Springbet



Once upon a time, in a place and a time far far away from where human civilization once were at, there sits the Woods of Spline. It is a magical forest that renews itself. Unseen cyborg creatures that resemble the most ordinary animals on earth, seem to form a mutual biosphere that sustains the living of the entire forest. Nobody knows who or what have altered them, yet there perhaps are much for humans of our times to learn from.
