GARMENT / COLLECTION

Synthetic Skins 1.0, 2.0



"What wakes you up in the morning?"


Summer 2023, I worked with 3D garment artist Nusi Quero. Since then he has been my biggest mentor. Anyone who has ever worked with him would celebrate the spontaneity in his creative process. He looks at the forms and figure to decide his next move, a very sensitive and hand-on process.

He made me finally understand, there's only one way to find out. Do it.

Synthetic Skins Collection 1.0

inflatable packaging, packaging tape, gap-filler expandable foam, sewing pins, latex

Synthetic Skins Collection 2.0

cling wrap, resin, gauze, wool, cotton, latex



This collection is an extension of previous material experimentations into the realm of spontaneous garment making and experimental photo-shoot.

The sculptural wearable hover between fashion, prosthesis, and artifact. It resist polish and permanence, instead embracing the rawness of process—immediacy, improvisation, and tactile estrangement.

From trash into tactile skins, the body is staged as an experimental archive of ecological entanglement.


"Where must beauty lies within? What are these impermanency trying to tell us?"
